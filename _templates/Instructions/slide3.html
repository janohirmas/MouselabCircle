Each type of information can have two possible values.

For some information, e.g. Personality, we calculated the average score across all participants.

The information displayed then indicates whether a person was below or above the average.

Below you can see what the two different values indicate for each information.

  • General Mental Ability (GMA):
    • High GMA
    • Low GMA
  • Personality (Conscientiousness):
    • High Consc.
    • Low Consc.
  • Unstructured Interview:
    • Good Interview
    • Bad Interview
  • Gender:
    • Female
    • Male
  • Number in sample :
    • Odd
    • Even
