    • General Mental Ability (GMA): measures the ability to process verbal information, numbers and graphs.
    • Personality (Conscientiousness): measures the tendency to be responsible, organized, hard-working, goal-directed, and to adhere to norms and rules.

    • Gender: Stated gender.
